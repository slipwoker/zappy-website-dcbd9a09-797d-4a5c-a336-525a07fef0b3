Garage Door Hardware
Categories & Components

MGM Garage Supply is your trusted industrial hardware supply source for professional-grade garage door components. Whether you're managing a repair, a full replacement, or a new installation, our warehouse carries the dependable parts contractors and installers rely on — built for performance, stocked for availability.

From heavy-duty torsion springs to precision-engineered tracks and mounting hardware, every component in our catalog meets the demands of daily commercial and residential use. Browse our six core product families below to find exactly what your project requires.

Springs

Torsion and extension springs engineered for precise counterbalance across residential and commercial door weights. Available in standard and high-cycle ratings.

Rollers

Steel and nylon rollers in 10-ball, 13-ball, and heavy-duty variants. Designed for smooth, quiet operation and long service life under frequent cycling.

Hinges

Numbered section hinges (#1 through #5) in galvanized and stainless steel. Properly matched hinges ensure panel alignment and prevent premature wear.

Tracks

Vertical and horizontal track sections in standard and low-headroom configurations. Precision-formed steel channels that guide roller movement through every cycle.

Cables

Galvanized lift cables and safety cables rated for residential and commercial loads. Properly tensioned cables are critical to balanced, safe door operation.

Mounting Hardware

Lag screws, anchor bolts, strut brackets, and jamb hardware for secure structural attachment. Every fastener spec'd for the load demands of heavy door assemblies.
